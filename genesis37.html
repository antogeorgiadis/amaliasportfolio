Genesis 37

A Sci-fi Odyssey

GENESIS 37, a groundbreaking immersive experience, took center stage on November 3, 2020, during the Talos: Science Fiction Festival of London. This unique performance unfolded at the prestigious Cockpit Auditorium and simultaneously ventured into the virtual realm via Zoom. Blurring the lines between reality and digital space, it delved deep into the clash of identities between humanity and the realm of cyborgs, exploring a pervasive sense of displacement.

A Dystopian Vision: Set in a hauntingly plausible near-future dystopia, "GENESIS 37" grapples with the aftermath of a deadly syndrome that claimed the lives of 75% of the female population and left the remainder unable to conceive. In this grim reality, the World's Advanced Neuroscience Department (W.A.N.D) embarked on a daring mission to save humanity through human cloning.

A World Reimagined: Before the performance, "GENESIS 37" introduced an interactive website for W.A.N.D. This website allowed the audience to immerse themselves further in the narrative, providing in-depth FAQs about cloning, opportunities to virtually meet the clones, and a glimpse into the brilliant minds behind this groundbreaking technology.

An Unsettling Exploration: The audience was granted a unique opportunity to step into this new world of possibilities, meet the brilliant minds behind the cloning technology, and even engage in interviews with the very first human clones. This immersive journey into a disorienting future was nothing short of a gripping and unsettling exploration of power, pleasure, and the essence of existence. "GENESIS 37" pushed the boundaries of traditional theatre, leaving audiences both captivated and unnerved in its polished presentation.
