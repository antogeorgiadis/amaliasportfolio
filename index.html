BIOGRAPHY

Amalia has broad experience in acting, directing, devising, coaching, and teaching.
She holds a Fine Arts Degree in Theatre Studies from the University of Peloponnese and a Master’s in Advanced Theatre Practice from the Royal Central School of Speech and Drama. She has worked as an actress, theatre play consultant, and creator/performer in the entertainment industry, focusing on bespoke events. Alongside her theatre career, she currently works as a Drama Teacher and Creative Educator in London, UK. Amalia is passionate about creating, directing, and participating in relatable performances that evoke deep feelings and take the audience on a unique, thought-provoking journey.
